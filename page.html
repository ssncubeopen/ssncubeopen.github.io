About



SSN CUBE OPEN 2017 is organised by Department of Mathematics, SSN College of Engineering,Chennai and will be held during 28th and 29th of July in association with the World Cube Association. The competition is open to all competitors of all nationalities and age group. No prior experience in WCA competitions is necessary, but competitors should be familiar with the regulation.Certain event based time limits will be in place in each category which will be announced on the spot.
